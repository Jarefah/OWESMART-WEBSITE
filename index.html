How it works?

Step 1: Consolidate Everything

Connect all your liabilities in one place—credit cards, personal loans, and even hidden Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) debt—for a single, intuitive view.

Step 2: Get Your AI Strategy

Our AI coach analyzes your cash flow and recommends the most cost-effective repayment strategy (Avalanche or Snowball) to maximize your interest savings.

Step 3: Track Your Progress & Win

Stay motivated with gamified trackers, milestone rewards, and personalized nudges that turn debt repayment into achievable goals.

Our Mission

We empower Malaysian Millennials, Gen Z, and SMEs to achieve faster, less stressful financial independence. We accomplish this by providing a structured, data-driven platform that simplifies, optimises, and motivates the debt management process.

Our Vision

OweSmart envisions a future where every individual and SME can achieve financial freedom with AI-driven insights, transparent debt visibility, and gamified financial discipline — fostering a financially resilient and confident society.
